Shoyu Butter Udon Noodles

Thick chewy noodles are combined with shiitake mushrooms and edamame beans coated in a buttery, umami sauce. This recipe comes together more quickly than take-out and is just as delicious! It's the perfect meal for busy weeknights.

  • 400 g precooked udon noodles
  • 2 Tbsp usalted vegan butter
  • 200 g shiitake mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 stalks green onion, sliced
  • 1 Tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 Tbsp mushroom stir-fry sauce
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 3/4 cup frozen shelled edamame
  1. Fill a saucepan with boiling water, and add the udon noodles. Cook for 2 minutes, then drain.
  2. Meanwhile, add the butter and mushrooms to a large pan on high heat. Cook until the mushrooms are lightly golden, about 4 minutes.
  3. Add the drained noodles, green onion, soy sauce, mushroom sauce, and garlic powder. Cook for 1 minute.
  4. Lastly, add the edamame beans and cook until heated through. Enjoy!
